Where I'm From

I was born in the beautiful Cherokee Nation of Oklahoma, where I spent the first 40 years of my life. Following a career in Emergency Services, my family and I have made our home on the east coast of North Carolina.

Just a few years ago, business led me to the east coast of North Carolina. What was scheduled to be a 7-10 day trip, turned into a 3-week trip, then multiple return trips. Finally, my family decided to make our home here. Although Oklahoma is our home, we enjoy life at the beach.
