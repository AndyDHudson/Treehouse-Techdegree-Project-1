Some of my Experience

Education Experience

I have always had an interest in technology. While in high-school, I studied Computer Programming at my local Vocational Technical School. I won’t tell you what year that was, but I will admit technology has changed a bit.

After completing High School, I went on to college at a small university in southern Oklahoma. There I studied Political Science while attending on a music scholarship.

While in college, I developed an interest in Fire and Emergency Medical Services which led me to join my local volunteer fire department after graduation. From there I attended the Oklahoma State University: Fire Service Training and three years of EMT and Paramedic education.

Work Experience

I began working when I was in high school. I landed my first job at the age of 15 years old. I landed my first “real” job while I was in college. I worked as a computer programmer for a local bank.

After college I began volunteering with my local volunteer fire department. After attending Fire Academy and Paramedic School, I began working with a fire department as a Professional Firefighter and Fire and Emergency Medical Service instructor with the University.

After leaving the fire department a few years ago, I became a business owner and began working with other small and start-up business.
